FAQ

Hard questions, answered clearly. No dodging.

Do you guarantee insurance premium reductions?

No. Anyone guaranteeing that is either reckless or lying. We focus on risk controls + documentation so insurers/boards can assess your position properly.

What makes you different from a normal contractor?

We productise the work, define scope tightly, and deliver an evidence pack. You don’t just get “work done” — you get a defensible record of risk controls.

Will you coordinate approvals and stakeholder comms?

We can support with templates and scheduling notes. Formal approvals (council, compliance, legal) remain the asset owner’s responsibility unless otherwise agreed in writing.

Do you consider ecological impacts?

Yes. Risk reduction shouldn’t equal ecological vandalism. We triage what stays, what goes, and why — and we document it.
